Password2 was developed by Forest Wilson to serve as a way to quickly generate secure passwords without needing to custom fit criteria like you would in other password generating websites. This will generate a proper length password with all necessary symbols and characters out of the box, cutting down on the total time it takes to get a new, secure password.

By default, Password2 will generate an 8 char string, containing lowercase and uppercase letters, as well as numbers and symbols. All ambiguous characters have been removed to make the password easy to recite and interpret over the phone, as well as over text. (ie. 1, I, l, 0, O, o) Generated passwords will render to the page in case you need to revisit a previous one.

I am working on adding more features, so if there is a something you would like me to add, shoot me a message and I'll try and put it in!
