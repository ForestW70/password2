Password2 was developed by Forest Wilson to serve as a way to quickly generate secure passwords without needing to custom fit criteria like you would in other password generating websites. This will generate a proper length password with all necessary symbols and characters out of the box, cutting down on the total time it takes to get a new, secure password.

By default, Password2 will generate an 8 char string, containing lowercase and uppercase letters, as well as numbers and symbols. All unfamiliar symbols and ambiguous text characters have been removed to make the password easy to recite and interpret over the phone, as well as over text (ie. 1, I, l, 0, O, o, {, }, ~, ect...). The previously generated password will be remembered, and can be accessed by the "Last password" hint button in case you need to revisit it.

*Remember to guide your user to the nearest password reset facility after using this generator.*
